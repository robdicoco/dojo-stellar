## ğŸš€ **About Dojo Stellar**

<p align="justify">
Dojo Stellar is a hands-on immersion organized by <strong>NearX</strong> (<a href="https://app.nearx.com.br" target="_blank">app.nearx.com.br</a>) for developers who wish to master the Stellar ecosystem and prepare for emerging opportunities in <strong>Web3</strong>.
</p>

<p align="justify">
The purpose of the dojo is to provide a dynamic and collaborative environment for participants to learn <strong>by doing</strong> how to develop decentralized applications using the Stellar blockchain. During the event, weekly challenges will be conducted that allow developers to deepen their technical knowledge, enhance their portfolios, and stand out in the blockchain and decentralized finance (<strong>DeFi</strong>) market.
</p>

---

## ğŸ¯ **Goals and Learning**

<p align="justify">
Dojo Stellar empowers participants to:
</p>

- **Understand the Stellar ecosystem** and its real-world applications.
- **Explore opportunities in Web3** and new business possibilities for 2025.
- **Work with Stellar code updates** and actively contribute to the ecosystem.
- **Develop practical projects** that can be used to enhance your professional portfolio.

<p align="justify">
The event is aimed both at beginner developers and those who already have experience in blockchain and wish to expand their knowledge of Stellar.
</p>
